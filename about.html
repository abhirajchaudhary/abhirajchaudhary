About Me

UX Designer & Researcher, UI Designer, Product Designer, Computer Engineer

I am a final year Undergrad student majoring in Computer Science from Vellore Institute of Technology, Tamil Nadu. I use tools such as designing posters, websites, UIs, UX to tap into the inner psychology of people. I am a big fan of Don Norman's work in cognitive science and am passionate about disruptive changes through the use of design and technology.

Currently, I am working towards bridging the skill gap worldwide by as a Product Design Intern at Ignitus which is a non-profit and works towards the upliftment of education equity worldwide by decentralizing research, skill-acquisition and employment opportunities.

To me the world is a big UX challenge and Design thinking is my solution.
